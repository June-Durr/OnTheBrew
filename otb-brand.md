## Overall Aesthetic

The brand is heritage Guatemalan coffee with a small-batch, family-roaster personality. The About page positions the company around Cecilia and Jonathan’s Guatemalan roots, microlot coffee sourcing, family tradition, quality, sustainability, and authenticity. (OTB)
Visual identity:
Clean Shopify storefront.
White negative space.
Classic blue serif wordmark.
Botanical coffee illustrations.
Guatemalan origin cues.
Warm coffee-brown product artwork.
Large real-world photography of the Brewmobile and coffee service.
Simple ecommerce UI with minimal decoration.
Homepage style:
Hero image with scenic Guatemala landscape and large blue wordmark.
Event-service messaging around the Brewmobile.
CTA language: “Request a Quote.”
Tone: approachable, local, community-oriented, handcrafted. (OTB)
Product-page style:
Minimal Shopify product layout.
Large square product artwork.
Uppercase roast names.
Brown/coffee backgrounds.
Guatemalan flag/origin graphics.
Simple price, variant, quantity, and cart controls.
Coffee tasting notes are direct and simple, such as citrus, maple syrup, roasted nuts, smooth finish. (OTB)
